Retorno

Please send back your unwanted items for a refund, and place a new order for the correct product. Check our return page for your return shipping options.

We do our best to refund the money to your account as fast as possible. Normally the money will be available in your bank account within one week after we have received your returned item.

The exchange and return policy are up to the individual retailer. We do not handle returns of products purchased from one of our retailers.

Some bundles offer a free product with the purchase of two or more specified products. Bundles can only be fully refunded if all items making up the bundle are returned. If one or more products within the bundle, including the free product, are not returned, we will deduct the value of each individual product that is not returned from your refund.
